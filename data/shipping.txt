Shipping Policy:
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days ($5.99 or free for orders over $50)
- Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($12.99)
- Next Day Shipping: Delivered next business day if ordered before 2 PM EST ($24.99)
- International Shipping: Available to most countries (7-14 business days, cost varies by location)
- All orders are processed within 24 hours Monday-Friday
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST may be processed the next business day
- Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day
- Tracking information is provided for all shipments via email

Shipping Restrictions:
- Certain products cannot be shipped internationally due to customs regulations
- We do not ship to P.O. boxes for Express or Next Day deliveries
- Additional customs fees may apply for international orders, which are the responsibility of the customer
- Some remote areas may experience longer delivery times
